This project's BIOES/BIO Viterbi decoder
(src/background/pii/bioes.ts) is a TypeScript re-implementation
patterned after the constrained Viterbi decoder in
openai/privacy-filter (https://github.com/openai/privacy-filter),
specifically the `opf/_core/` module.

No source code was copied. The algorithm and structural pattern
(linear-chain transition scoring, BIOES emission constraints,
dynamic-programming forward pass with backtrace) are well-known
in NER literature and are re-derived in our implementation.
